Since 2009

From One Oven to Many Tables

BakeryStore was founded in 2009 by Maria and Thomas, who believed truly great bread could only be made with time, patience, and the best ingredients. What began as a weekend market stall quickly became a beloved institution.

We source our flour from a heritage mill, our butter from a small dairy farm, and our inspiration from European artisan baking traditions. Every loaf is shaped by hand and baked to order.

We don't cut corners. We don't rush fermentation. We believe the world tastes better with good bread ‚Äî and we bake that belief into everything we make.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

Every great bakery has a story. Here's ours.

2009

The First Loaf

Maria and Thomas bake their first sourdough at a Sunday farmers market. It sells out in 20 minutes.

2012

Opening Our First Shop

We sign a lease on a tiny Baker Street storefront. One oven, a second-hand display case, and a chalkboard menu.

2016

Expanding the Menu

Aisha joins the team and launches our pastry range. The cinnamon rolls become a city-wide obsession.

2020

Launching Online Delivery

We launch delivery to bring fresh bread to customers' doors. 10,000 loaves delivered in the first month.

Now
