From One Oven to Many Tables

BakeryStore was founded in 2009 by Maria and Thomas, a couple who believed that truly great bread could only be made with time, patience, and the best ingredients available. What began as a weekend market stall quickly grew into a beloved neighbourhood institution.

We source our flour from a single heritage mill, our butter from a small dairy farm, and our inspiration from the rich traditions of European artisan baking. Every loaf, roll, and pastry is shaped by hand and baked to order.

We don't cut corners. We don't rush our fermentation. We believe the world tastes better with good bread in it ‚Äî and we bake that belief into everything we make.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

Every great bakery has a story. Here's ours.

2009

The First Loaf

Maria and Thomas bake their first sourdough at a Sunday farmers market. It sells out in 20 minutes. They come back the following weekend with twice as much.

2012

Opening Our First Shop

We sign the lease on a tiny storefront on Baker Street. Just one oven, a second-hand display case, and a handwritten chalkboard menu.

2016

Expanding the Menu

Aisha joins the team and launches our pastry range. The cinnamon rolls become a city-wide obsession almost overnight.

2020

Launching Online Delivery

During a difficult year for everyone, we launch our delivery service to bring fresh bread to our customers' doors. We deliver 10,000 loaves in the first month.

Now

Still Baking, Still Growing

Today we serve thousands of customers a week across the city. But every morning starts the same way ‚Äî with flour on our hands and love in the oven.
